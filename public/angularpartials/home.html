Something fun you can do with angular is to use the controller page to quickly edit textlines in the partial en-mass. For instance, {{bacon}} , this word can be edited using the scope fucntion for controller page. On the controller page it would look like this, "$scope.bacon = 'thething' ", where "thething" is what you want to show up on the partial page. In this case "thething" is the word "red".

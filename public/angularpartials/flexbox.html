For this div we will see the effects of justify content and align items on the postioning of div contents.

The justify-content function of Flexbox adjusts things from right to left, while the align-items will adjust things from top to bottom.

This is justify-content: flex-end and align-items: flex-end

It is also important to note that you must add the "display: flex" line to all css classes leading to the one where you want the Flexbox css active.
